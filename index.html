The daily ritual

Four acts, every morning

I

The fire

The oven wakes before the roosters. By the time the city opens its eyes, the crust already has its color.

II

The wait

The dough takes the hours it asks for — never the ones we'd prefer. Patience is the only secret ingredient.

III

The tear

That first pull — the crackle, the steam, the cheese that won't let go. Nobody waits until they get home.

IV

The table

Bread is only bread until it's shared. Φιλοξενία — the table is set, and you're already invited.

Questions, answered

Where is Hornofino located?

We're an artisan bakery and café in San Juan, Puerto Rico, baking fresh bread and pastries daily — find every address on the locations page.

What do you bake?

Pan de agua, pan sobao, mallorcas, quesitos, croquetas, donas, flanes and full breakfast plates — all made from scratch, every day. There's also a fine-dining side: Pane e Pasta.

What are your hours?

Open daily from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Bread is out of the oven by 7:00; pastries follow through the morning. Favorites sell out — come early.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes. Order online for same-day pickup at any bakery, or delivery within the San Juan area. For catering and large orders, reach out and we'll take it from there.
