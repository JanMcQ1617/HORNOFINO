Our Story

One oven, the Greek art of welcome, and bread made the old, patient way.

Since the first loaf

A table set in the old way

Hornofino began with a single stone oven and a belief that good bread needs only time, fire, and honest hands. We bake the way our grandparents did along the coast — slowly, patiently, and with φιλοξενία, the Greek art of generous welcome.

No shortcuts. No noise. Just flour, water, salt, and the warmth of the oven carrying the scent of morning through the door. What leaves the oven each dawn is sold that same day — never the next.

What we believe

Three things, kept simple

🜂

Fire

A stone oven lit before dawn. Heat does what no machine can — a crust with character.

⏳

Time

Long, slow fermentation. We let the dough take the hours it needs, never the ones we'd prefer.

☍

Φιλοξενία

Generous welcome. A bakery is a table — everyone who walks in is a guest, not a transaction.
