The table after dark

A trattoria grown from a bakery

Pane e Pasta began the way the best things do — with leftover fire and a good idea. The bread was already the best in the neighborhood; the kitchen simply kept going after the pastries were gone.

Pasta rolled the same morning. Sauces that simmer while you sit. A short, serious wine list, and a room that fills with the smell of garlic, butter and the last of the day's loaves. Slow food, the way it was meant to be shared.

Pane · Pasta · Vino — la vita è bella.
