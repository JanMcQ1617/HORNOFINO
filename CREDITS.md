## 3D model — Atlas hero

The Atlas statue is a photogrammetry scan of the **Farnese Atlas** (2nd-century
Roman marble, Museo Archeologico Nazionale di Napoli), from the **Scan the World**
initiative (MyMiniFactory), decimated for web use. Licensed **CC BY 4.0** —
attribution required.
